# Signing in

Panthera Insight uses a **two-way sign-in** flow. Your first sign-in is by **email code** (also called a "magic link" or OTP); after that you may set a **password** and use **biometric unlock** for quicker access on the same device.

## First-time sign-in (email code)

1. Open the app and tap **Sign in**.
2. Enter the email address Panthera has on file for you.
3. Tap **Send code**. A 6-digit code will be emailed to you within a few seconds.
4. Open the email and enter the code in the app.
5. Once verified, the app will load your profile and take you to your reports.

## Setting a password

After your first successful sign-in, you can set a password for faster sign-in next time.

1. Go to **Settings → Security → Set password**.
2. Enter a password that meets the on-screen strength requirements.
3. Confirm the password.

Once set, you can sign in by entering your email address and password, instead of waiting for a code.

## Biometric unlock

If your device has Face ID, Touch ID or Android fingerprint enabled, the app will offer to use it as a quick way to re-open the app on the **same device**.

- **What biometric unlock does:** lets you re-open the app without re-entering your password each time, as long as your sign-in session is still valid.
- **What it does not do:** it does not replace your password. If your session expires, you will need to sign in again with your password (or by email code).

## Sign-out

To sign out, go to **Settings → Sign out**. This clears your session on the current device. To sign out of every device you have signed in on, use **Settings → Security → Sign out everywhere** — see [Managing your account](account-management.md) for details.

## If you cannot sign in

See [Troubleshooting](troubleshooting.md#sign-in-problems) for the common issues — code not arriving, code rejected, "too many attempts" message, and so on.
