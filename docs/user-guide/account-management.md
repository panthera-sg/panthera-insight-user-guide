# Managing your account

This page covers the day-to-day account settings in the app.

## Updating your details

Most account details — the email address Panthera has on file, your name, which client accounts you can access — are managed by your Panthera relationship manager, not from inside the app. If anything needs to change, contact your relationship manager.

The app's **Settings** screen lets you control:

- Whether **biometric unlock** is on.
- Whether you have a **password** set for faster sign-in.
- Notifications (if your version of the app uses them).

## Changing your password

If you have set a password (see [Signing in](signing-in.md#setting-a-password)) you can change or remove it from **Settings → Security**.

- **Change password:** asks for the current password, then a new one.
- **Remove password:** disables password sign-in. You will still be able to sign in using the email-code flow.

## Signing out

- **Sign out** (Settings → Sign out): clears your session on the current device only.
- **Sign out everywhere** (Settings → Security → Sign out everywhere): clears your sessions on every device you have signed in on. Use this if you suspect unauthorised access, if you have lost a device, or simply if you want a clean slate.

After signing out everywhere, the next sign-in will require a fresh email code.

## Deleting your account

You can delete your Panthera Insight account from inside the app:

1. Go to **Settings → Delete account**.
2. Read the on-screen description of what is removed (and what is retained for regulatory reasons).
3. Confirm.

The app calls Panthera's server-side deletion routine while you wait. On success it signs you out and clears any cached data on the device.

!!! danger "Deletion is irreversible"
    Once your account is deleted your email is removed from the access allow-list, and you cannot sign in again unless Panthera invites you afresh as a new account holder.

## What is retained after deletion

For regulatory reasons (Panthera is a MAS-licensed entity) the firm must keep certain client records — including portfolio reports prepared for you and the profile information against which they were prepared — for the periods set by applicable law. These records are not generated by you and remain in Panthera's books after your access is deleted. The [Delete your account](../legal/delete-account.md) page describes this in detail.
