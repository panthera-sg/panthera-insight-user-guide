## About the app

Panthera Insight is provided to existing clients of Panthera International Asia Capital Pte. Ltd. — an entity regulated under a Singapore Monetary Authority (MAS) licence (CMS101735). The app does not process payments, hold funds, or transmit money. Reports are produced by Panthera's portfolio operations team and made available to the named client through the app.
