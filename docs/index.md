# User guide

Welcome to the Panthera Insight user guide. This is the place to learn how to install the app, sign in to your account, find your portfolio reports, and manage your account settings.

The app is designed to be simple: it shows you your latest report and lets you browse historical reports prepared by Panthera's portfolio operations team. There are no trades to place, no buttons that move money, and no advice generated by the app itself.

## About the app

Panthera Insight is provided to existing clients of Panthera International Asia Capital Pte. Ltd. — an entity regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) under Capital Markets Services Licence **CMS101735** for fund management. The app does not process payments, hold funds, or transmit money. Reports are produced by Panthera's portfolio operations team and made available to the named client through the app.

## Who to contact

For anything specific to your portfolio or the contents of your reports, please contact your Panthera relationship manager directly.

For app-related questions (sign-in problems, an error message, suspicious activity on your account), email [pantherainsight@gmail.com](mailto:pantherainsight@gmail.com).
