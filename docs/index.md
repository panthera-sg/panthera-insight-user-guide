# User guide

Welcome to the Panthera Insight user guide. This is the place to learn how to install the app, sign in to your account, find your portfolio reports, download PDFs, and manage your account settings.

The app is designed to be simple: it shows you your latest report, lets you browse and download historical reports prepared by Panthera's portfolio operations team, and gives you control over a few security and appearance settings. There are no trades to place, no buttons that move money, and no advice generated by the app itself.

## About the app

Panthera Insight is provided to existing clients of Panthera International Asia Capital Pte. Ltd. — an entity regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) under Capital Markets Services Licence **CMS101735** for fund management. The app does not process payments, hold funds, or transmit money. Reports are produced by Panthera's portfolio operations team and made available to the named client through the app.
