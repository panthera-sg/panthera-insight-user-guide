# User guide

Welcome to the Panthera Insight user guide. This is the place demonstrating how to install the app, sign in to your account, find your portfolio reports, and manage your account settings.

The app is designed to be simple: it shows you your latest report and lets you browse historical reports prepared by Panthera Singapore's team. There are no trades to place, no buttons that initiate any transaction, and no advice generated by the app itself.

<div class="callout">
This app provides information only and does not constitute financial advice or a recommendation to invest. Past performance is not a reliable indicator of future results. Investments can fall as well as rise.
</div>

## About the app

Panthera Insight is provided to existing clients of Panthera International Asia Capital Pte. Ltd. — an entity regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS) under Capital Markets Services Licence **CMS101735** for fund management. The app does not process payments, hold funds, or transmit money. Reports are produced by Panthera Singapore's team and made available to the named client through the app.

## Who to contact

For app-related questions (sign-in problems, an error message, suspicious activity on your account), email [pantherainsight@gmail.com](mailto:pantherainsight@gmail.com).

For privacy notices, terms of service and account-deletion procedures, visit the [legal & privacy site](https://panthera-sg.github.io/panthera-insight-legal/).
