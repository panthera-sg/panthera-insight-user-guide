## About the app

Panthera Insight is a **read-only portfolio reporting** application provided to existing clients of Panthera International Asia Capital Pte. Ltd. The app does not process payments, hold funds, or transmit money. Reports are produced by Panthera's portfolio operations team and made available to the named client through the app.
