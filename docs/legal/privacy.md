# Privacy Notice

<p class="lede">Last updated: 14 May 2026.</p>

This notice describes how **Panthera International Asia Capital Pte. Ltd.** ("Panthera", "we", "us") collects and processes personal data through the Panthera Insight mobile app. It is written to satisfy Singapore's Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (as amended in 2020) ("PDPA"), the UK General Data Protection Regulation and the Data Protection Act 2018 ("UK GDPR"), Canada's Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act ("PIPEDA") and Quebec's Act respecting the protection of personal information in the private sector ("Quebec Law 25").

## 1. Who we are

Panthera International Asia Capital Pte. Ltd. is the data controller for personal data processed via the Panthera Insight mobile app. We are licensed and regulated by the Monetary Authority of Singapore (MAS).

## 2. Data Protection Officer

Our Data Protection Officer ("DPO") can be reached at [pantherainsight@gmail.com](mailto:pantherainsight@gmail.com). You may contact the DPO with any question about this notice, to exercise the rights described in section 7, or to report a suspected privacy incident.

## 3. What we collect

- **Account data:** email address (used to issue one-time sign-in codes), and any name on file with our portfolio operations team.
- **Reporting data:** portfolio reports prepared for you by Panthera and made available through the app, including positions, valuations and historical performance.
- **Diagnostic data:** crash reports and basic device information, automatically scrubbed of email addresses, tokens and identifiers before they leave the device.

We do not collect advertising identifiers, location data, contacts, photos, microphone or camera data. We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

## 4. Why we process it

- To authenticate you and deliver your portfolio reports (performance of contract).
- To investigate crashes and improve reliability (legitimate interest in maintaining a working app).
- To comply with our obligations under applicable financial services and data protection law (legal obligation).

## 5. Who else processes it

We use a small number of sub-processors. Each is bound by a Data Processing Agreement.

- **Supabase** — authentication, database and encrypted file storage. Data is hosted in Supabase's regional datacentres (typically Singapore for our deployment); the Privacy Notice will be updated if the region changes.
- **Sentry** — crash reporting. Routed through Sentry's EU region (ingest.de.sentry.io). PII is removed by an on-device scrubber before any event leaves your phone.
- **Apple, Google** — app distribution. Apple and Google do not receive your reporting data; they only receive what they need to deliver app updates and process App Store / Play Store interactions you initiate yourself.

## 6. International transfers

Personal data may be processed outside your country of residence when our sub-processors operate from facilities in other jurisdictions. Where data leaves Singapore, the United Kingdom or the EEA, we rely on the data-transfer provisions in our sub-processor agreements (PDPA Transfer Limitation Obligation, and UK / EU Standard Contractual Clauses where applicable).

## 7. Your rights

You may at any time:

- Ask what personal data we hold about you;
- Ask us to correct inaccurate data;
- Ask us to delete your account and associated data (see [Delete your account](delete-account.md) for the in-app and web paths);
- Withdraw your consent to optional processing;
- Lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority — the Personal Data Protection Commission of Singapore (PDPC) in Singapore, the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) in the United Kingdom, the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada (OPC) or, for Quebec residents, the *Commission d'accès à l'information* (CAI).

Requests should be sent to the DPO mailbox above. We aim to respond within 30 days; complex requests may take up to a further 60 days, in which case we will tell you.

## 8. Retention

Account data is kept while your account is active. Reporting data is retained per the record-keeping requirements applicable to a MAS-licensed entity. When you delete your account, account data and reporting data are removed from our active systems; sub-processor backups may retain copies for a limited period before they are overwritten.

## 9. Children

The app is provided to identified adult clients of Panthera. It is not directed at children and we do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18.

## 10. Notice for residents of Canada

For residents of Canada this notice is written to satisfy the federal **Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act ("PIPEDA")** and, for Quebec residents, the **Act respecting the protection of personal information in the private sector ("Quebec Law 25")**. The rights described in section 7 apply to Canadian residents in addition to any rights granted by federal or provincial law.

**Privacy officer.** The PIPEDA-required individual accountable for our personal information practices is the same Data Protection Officer named in section 2 of this notice ([pantherainsight@gmail.com](mailto:pantherainsight@gmail.com)). The same address serves as the contact for Quebec Law 25 access, correction, de-indexation and portability requests.

**Cross-border transfers.** Personal information processed through the Panthera Insight app is hosted by Supabase (typically in the Singapore region; the regional choice is documented in section 5) and Sentry (EU region). Quebec Law 25 requires explicit notice that data may be processed outside of Canada — this paragraph constitutes that notice.

**Automated decision-making.** The app does not make decisions about you by purely automated means. Reports are prepared by Panthera staff; the app only displays them.

**Breach reporting.** Where a privacy breach creates a real risk of significant harm we report to the Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada ("OPC") under PIPEDA and, for Quebec residents, to the *Commission d'accès à l'information* ("CAI") within 72 hours under Quebec Law 25. Affected individuals are notified at the same time.

**Sale of personal information.** We do not sell personal information and we do not share it for behavioural advertising. We do not use third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.

## 11. Changes to this notice

If we materially change how we process personal data, we will update the date at the top of this page and, where appropriate, notify you in-app. Continued use of the app after a change indicates acceptance of the updated notice.
